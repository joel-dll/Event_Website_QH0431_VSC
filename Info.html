| Getting here |
We are always trying to encourage more
people to come to the festival
by bike. If you are driving to the Festival,
you will need a car parking pass.
If you have a pass, put the
sticker on the windscreen
well before you reach the Festival site.
Postcode: SE22 0SH

| Lost Property |
First of all, thank you to everyone
who handed something in at last year
festival, you made a lot
of people very happy!
Please hand in any items to the
Information points around site,
which will then be brought to
the Lost and Found Services.
You can also email to lostproperty@peckhambeatsfestival.co.uk
