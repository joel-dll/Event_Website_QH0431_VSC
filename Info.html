| Getting here |
We are always trying to encourage more people to come to the festival by bike. If you are driving to the Festival, you will need a car parking pass. If you have a pass, put the sticker on the windscreen well before you reach the Festival site.
Postcode: SE22 0SH

| Care and Safety |
We do not accept any discrimination towards any individual or groups of individuals at any of our festivals. Irrespective of the background of the individual,be that gender, race, disability, age, religion or belief,sexual orientation, gender reassignment, marital or civil partnership status, pregnancy or maternity. We are proud of the diverse nature of our festivals and their customers, if you see or experience anyone undermining this no matter the severity please report it immediately to one of our onsite staff.

| Lost Property |
First of all, thank you to everyone who handed something in at last year festival, you made a lot of people very happy! Please hand in any items to the Information points around site, which will then be brought to the Lost and Found Services.
You can also email to lostproperty@peckhambeatsfestival.co.uk
